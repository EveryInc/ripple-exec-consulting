# Quarterly Board Content Generator

## Prompt

I'm President of Ripple. Every quarter, I spend significant time preparing content for our board meetings. I need to tell the story of our business results and key highlights, create slide content, and draft talking points that I can then edit.

Using the attached `q1_2026_results.csv` and `q1_highlights.md`, generate a complete board presentation package:

1. **Quarterly Narrative** (1-2 pages)
   Write the "story of the quarter" - not just what happened, but why it matters. This should read as a compelling executive narrative that connects our results to our strategy. Cover:
   - Overall business performance vs. plan
   - Product line highlights and lowlights
   - Market position and competitive dynamics
   - Acquisition integration progress
   - Regulatory milestones
   - What's working and what needs attention

2. **Slide Content** (8-10 slides worth of content, formatted as structured text I can paste into slides)
   - Slide 1: Q1 2026 Headline - 3 key takeaways
   - Slide 2: Financial Summary - revenue, expenses, EBITDA, cash position
   - Slide 3: Revenue by Business Line - breakdown with trends
   - Slide 4: Ripple Payments performance
   - Slide 5: RLUSD & Stablecoin momentum
   - Slide 6: Acquisition Integration scorecard
   - Slide 7: Regulatory & Market Expansion
   - Slide 8: Competitive Landscape
   - Slide 9: Key Risks & Mitigations
   - Slide 10: Outlook & Priorities for Q2

3. **Talking Points** - For each slide, provide 3-5 bullet points of what I should say (not what's on the slide). Include anticipated board questions and suggested responses.

**Tone:** Confident but honest. Boards respect leaders who own the bad news alongside the good. Be specific with numbers. No hand-waving.

Output as a single well-formatted HTML document with clear sections I can work through.
