# Weekly Leadership Update Drafter

## Prompt

I'm SVP of People, Places, and IT at Ripple. Every week I send an update to BG (CEO) covering what's happening across my function. Pulling this together from multiple sources takes 1-2 hours. I want AI to draft the first version.

Using the attached source files:
- `people_leads_update.md` (weekly updates from regional people leads)
- `slack_highlights.md` (key Slack messages from the week)
- `weekly_metrics.csv` (people/IT metrics)

Draft a weekly update email to BG that follows this format:

**Subject:** Weekly People, Places & IT Update - W/E [date]

**Structure:**
1. **TL;DR** (3 bullets max) - The three most important things BG needs to know this week
2. **People & Talent**
   - Headcount changes (hires, departures, open reqs)
   - Key hires or departures worth noting
   - Acquisition team integration status
   - Any retention risks or wins
3. **Culture & Engagement**
   - Pulse survey highlights (if available)
   - ERG activity
   - Notable wins or concerns
4. **IT & Security**
   - Claude/AI rollout status
   - Any security incidents or near-misses
   - Infrastructure updates
5. **Places & Workplace**
   - Office updates (openings, closures, renovations)
   - Upcoming events or visits
6. **Needs Your Attention** - Anything that requires BG's decision, visibility, or action
7. **Looking Ahead** - What's coming next week

**Tone:** Direct, concise, no fluff. BG skims these. Lead with what matters. Flag decisions needed clearly. Celebrate wins briefly. Don't bury bad news.

Output the email as plain text ready to paste into Gmail. Also generate a 3-bullet Slack summary for the #exec-team channel.
