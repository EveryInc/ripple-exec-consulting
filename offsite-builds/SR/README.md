# SR — VP, Strategic Initiatives

## Background
SR is VP of Strategic Initiatives at Ripple, reporting to EVM. He drives global expansion, M&A integration coordination, and executive programs across BD, Corp Dev, Corp Strategy & Ops, Marketing, and Social Impact. Previously Chief of Staff and Senior Director of Strategic Initiatives at Ripple; prior to that, led marketing program management at Credit Karma. 18 years of experience in strategic execution and operational scale.

His role is inherently cross-functional — he's the connective tissue between workstreams, teams, and leadership. The exercises below are designed around that synthesis-and-coordination pattern.

## Session Build

### Exercise 01 — Cross-Functional Initiative Tracker
**Complexity:** Medium | **Estimated time:** 25-35 min

Synthesizes status updates from multiple strategic workstreams (M&A integrations, market expansion, partnerships, executive programs) into a single view with blockers, dependencies, owners, and escalation flags. Designed for someone managing 5+ concurrent initiatives across different teams.

### Exercise 02 — Executive Program Follow-Up Engine
**Complexity:** Easy-Medium | **Estimated time:** 20-30 min

After exec sessions, offsites, or board meetings, tracks commitments, action items, and follow-ups across participants. Generates accountability reports and nudge drafts for items going stale. Directly relevant to what SR does after events like this offsite.

## Facilitator Notes
- **Support level:** SR is Ripple internal support for this session, not a primary participant. These exercises are for him to build during downtime or after supporting RN.
- **Context:** He knows the internal systems and connector landscape better than anyone in the room. If he builds, he'll move fast.
- **If time is tight:** Exercise 02 is the quicker win and directly useful for post-session follow-up from this very offsite.
