# KH — SVP Marketing and Comms

## Survey Response
- **Tools interested in:** Claude Chat, Claude Cowork
- **Tasks to hand off to AI:**
  1. Inbox searches - uses email as a filing system, wants quick search/organization
  2. Edits - reviews everything from emails to press releases to tweets to event copy
  3. Contracts and POs - signs/approves dozens weekly, needs fraud scanning and summarization

## Session Build

### Session Flow (Recommended)
1. **Start with Exercise 01 — Contract & PO Reviewer** (~15 min) — Quick win. Tangible, practical, low creative stakes. Builds confidence.
2. **Move to Exercise 02 — Editorial Assistant with Tone/Voice Training** (~30 min) — The deeper, more personally relevant build. Directly addresses her concern that "AI sounds robotic" by showing how to train Claude on her voice.

### Exercise 01 — Contract & PO Reviewer (Quick Win)
**Complexity:** Easy-Medium

Scans vendor contracts and purchase orders, flags potential red flags (unusual terms, missing clauses, signs of fraud), and produces a structured summary with key terms, total cost, payment schedule, and a recommended approve/escalate/reject decision.

### Exercise 02 — Editorial Assistant with Tone/Voice Training (Primary Build)
**Complexity:** Easy-Medium

Takes draft content across formats (press releases, executive emails, social posts, event copy, brand campaigns) and provides structured editorial feedback. **Now includes a tone/voice training section** that teaches KH how to feed Claude writing samples and set voice guidelines so edits match her style — not generic AI prose.

Includes optional `sample_voice_guide.md` — a mini Ripple comms style guide she can feed to Claude during the exercise.

### Optional Deep-Dive (Stretch / Follow-Up)

#### 3. Comms Inbox Organizer
Searches and categorizes email threads by topic, urgency, and action needed. Produces a daily digest view of what needs attention, organized by category (media requests, vendor follow-ups, event logistics, executive approvals, etc.).

## Facilitator Notes
- **Support level:** Needs support. KH is the most AI-reluctant participant — her core concern is "AI sounds robotic."
- **The tone/voice section is designed specifically for her.** The before/after calibration (generic AI edit vs. voice-matched edit) is the key "aha" moment. Make sure she gets there.
- **Don't start with editorial.** The Contract/PO reviewer is a safe, low-stakes win that builds confidence before tackling the more personal editorial exercise.
- **Natalia should prioritize KH** during individual build time, along with ML and SA.
