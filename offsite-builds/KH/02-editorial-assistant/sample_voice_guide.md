# Ripple Communications Voice Guide

## Core Principles
- **Professional, warm, direct.** We sound like a confident enterprise partner, not a startup pitching VCs.
- **Clarity over cleverness.** Every sentence should be understood on first read.
- **Substance over hype.** Let the product and numbers speak. No empty superlatives.

## We Say / We Don't Say

| Instead of... | We say... |
|--------------|-----------|
| "Ripple is a leading provider of..." | "Ripple enables..." or "Ripple powers..." |
| "excited to announce" | Just announce it. The news speaks for itself. |
| "game-changing" / "revolutionary" | Describe what actually changed and for whom |
| "crypto" (generic) | "digital assets," "enterprise blockchain," or name the specific asset (XRP, RLUSD) |
| "synergies" | Name the specific benefit: "shared customers," "combined product offering" |
| "thrilled to partner with" | "Ripple and [Partner] are launching..." |
| "leveraging blockchain technology" | Describe what the technology actually does for the customer |

## Headlines & Subject Lines
- **Action-oriented and specific.** Not "Ripple Announces Partnership" but "Ripple and Santander Launch Brazil-Mexico Payment Corridor"
- **Lead with the outcome**, not the company name when possible
- **Include a number or specificity** when available: "$500M," "30 new markets," "40% faster"

## Numbers & Data
- Always provide context. Not "$500M market cap" but "$500M market cap, doubling since launch in December"
- Use comparisons to make numbers meaningful: "Processing $2B daily — equivalent to [relatable comparison]"
- Round appropriately for the audience. Board deck: exact. Press release: rounded.

## Tone Calibration by Format
| Format | Tone |
|--------|------|
| Press release | Authoritative, factual, measured |
| Executive email (internal) | Direct, conversational, candid |
| Social post (LinkedIn) | Professional, forward-looking, concise |
| Social post (X/Twitter) | Sharper, punchier, still professional |
| Event copy | Energetic but not breathless |
| Board materials | Precise, structured, no filler |

## Brand Positioning
- Ripple is **enterprise infrastructure**, not speculative crypto
- We serve **banks, financial institutions, and enterprises** — not retail traders
- Our products solve **real business problems** (cross-border payments, treasury management, custody, stablecoin operations)
- We're a **regulated, compliance-first** company operating in 50+ jurisdictions
